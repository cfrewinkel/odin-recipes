Grilled Chicken Ranch Wraps with Balsamic Tomatoes & Cucumbers

Description

Ranch seasoned chicken thats wrapped with balsamic tomatoes and freshly cut cucumbers. Grilled to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 (5 oz) pkg baby spinach
  • 1 ½ lb chicken breasts, boneless skinless
  • 1 English cucumber
  • 4 large flour tortillas
  • ½ (8 oz) block mozzarella cheese
  • 2 tomatoes
  1. FHolding a knife parallel to the cutting board, cut the chicken in half horizontally to form thin fillets. Pat dry with paper towel and season both sides with salt and pepper.
  2. When the grill pan or skillet is hot, drizzle with oil and add fillets. Grill until cooked through, about 3-4 minutes per side. When the chicken is done, set aside on a plate and cover with aluminum foil. Wipe out and reserve pan for use later.
  3. Wash and dry the cucumber and tomatoes. Cut the tomatoes into wedges and the cucumber into thin, half moons; set aside.
  4. Add vinegar to a small saucepan and bring to a boil over medium-high heat. Once boiling, reduce to medium-low and continue to simmer, uncovered, until reduced by half, 5-7 minutes; remove from heat and set aside.
  5. Combine mayonnaise and spices in a small bowl; whisk to combine the ranch dressing.
  6. Wash and dry spinach. (Skip if it came pre-washed.)
  7. Lay the tortillas on a flat surface and divide spinach, chicken, grated cheese, and ranch dressing between them. Roll tightly to close.
  8. Place wraps on the grill and cook, rotating every 1-2 minutes, until tortilla is crispy and golden. Cut each wrap in half.
