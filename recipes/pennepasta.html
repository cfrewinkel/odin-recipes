Penne Pasta with Tuna & Tomato Sauce

Description

Penne pasta with chunk light tuna and diced tomatoes. Sprinkled with parmesan cheese.

Ingredients

  • 2 (6 oz) cans chunk light tuna in water
  • 2 (14.5 oz) cans diced tomatoes
  • 4 cloves garlic
  • 1 oz Parmesan cheese
  • 12 oz penne pasta
  • 1 medium yellow onion
  1. Fill a large pot halfway with water, cover, and bring to a boil. Uncover, add salt and pasta, and stir for a few seconds. Cook until desired firmness, 8-10 minutes. Drain.
  2. Heat a sauté pan over medium heat.
  3. Peel and small dice onion. Peel and mince garlic.
  4. Coat bottom of pan with oil. Add onion and, stirring frequently, cook until softened, 3-4 minutes.
  5. Grate ¼ cup of Parmesan.
  6. Drain tuna and stir into the sauce. Cook for an additional 2-3 minutes.
  7. Add pasta and toss to coat.
  8. To serve, place pasta in a bowl and sprinkle with Parmesan. Enjoy!
